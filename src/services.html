Our Services

Engineering Services with Clear Scope & Pricing

Every engagement is scoped to a specific deliverable. No open-ended hourly billing (except when you want it). We focus on mechanical and plumbing engineering for commercial and institutional projects.

01

MEP Peer Review

An independent, third-party review of mechanical and plumbing construction documents. We check code compliance (IMC, IPC, ASHRAE 90.1), coordination with architectural and structural drawings, equipment sizing, and constructability — drawing on years of design-build experience to catch issues before they become change orders.

  • Marked-up plan review with comment log
  • Code compliance checklist (IMC, IPC, ASHRAE)
  • Constructability assessment
  • Equipment sizing verification
  • Written summary report
Starting at $2,500
Request a Review
02

Energy Compliance

Complete ASHRAE 90.1 and California Title 24 energy compliance modeling and documentation. We produce permit-ready packages using IESVE — the same tool we've used for over a decade across laboratory, higher education, and commercial projects. Familiar with South Bay and greater LA authority-having-jurisdiction (AHJ) processes.

  • IESVE energy model (10+ years experience)
  • ASHRAE 90.1 compliance documentation
  • Title 24 compliance forms and certificates
  • Permit-ready submission package
  • AHJ coordination support
Starting at $3,500
Start Compliance Work
03

Schematic MEP Design

30% schematic mechanical and plumbing design for new construction and major renovations. Includes a basis-of-design narrative, equipment layouts, preliminary load calculations, and enough detail for early cost estimating. Particularly experienced with labs, vivariums, and fume hood-intensive facilities — environments where air quality, pressurization, and exhaust design are non-negotiable.

  • Basis of design narrative
  • Equipment layouts and schedules
  • Preliminary heating/cooling load calculations
  • Plumbing riser diagrams
  • ROM cost estimating support
Starting at $8,000
Discuss Your Project
04

Expert Consulting

Hourly engineering consulting for when you need targeted expertise without a full project engagement. Available for HVAC system assessments, plumbing and piping analysis, lab and vivarium system reviews, fume hood exhaust design, campus utility infrastructure, and energy study feasibility. Also available through expert networks (GLG, Guidepoint, AlphaSights, Tegus).

  • HVAC system assessments and troubleshooting
  • Lab, vivarium, and fume hood system consulting
  • Energy study feasibility analysis
  • Due diligence technical reviews
  • Expert network engagements
$300 / hour
Schedule a Consultation
05

CFD Analysis

Computational fluid dynamics modeling for complex airflow environments where standard engineering calculations aren't enough. We simulate real-world conditions to validate ventilation strategies, optimize airflow patterns, and identify turbulence issues before construction — reducing risk and avoiding costly field modifications.

  • Underground garage ventilation strategies & jet fan layouts
  • Paint booth laminar flow design & validation
  • Clean room airflow modeling & classification verification
  • Fume hood face velocity & turbulence analysis
  • Thermal comfort & contaminant dispersion studies
Starting at $5,000
Discuss CFD Scope
